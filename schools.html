For Schools

Bring Real Technology
into Your Classrooms

Roboden partners with schools to deliver hands-on robotics workshops and full STEAM lab setups — turning curious students into confident builders.

How It Works

Simple. Practical. Effective.

We handle everything — you just open your doors. Here's how a Roboden robotics workshop happens at your school.

STEP 01
Free Introductory Workshop
We visit your school and conduct a free robotics demonstration session for students. It's hands-on, exciting, and designed to spark curiosity — at zero cost to the school.
STEP 02
Student Registration
Interested students register for the full workshop program. We coordinate directly with the school to plan batch sizes, scheduling, and venue within your campus.
STEP 03
Full Robotics Workshop
We conduct the complete workshop at your school. Our trainer brings all hardware kits. Students build real projects from scratch — circuits, code, sensors, and actuators.
STEP 04
Certification
Every student who completes the workshop receives an official Roboden certificate — a recognition of their practical achievement and engineering skills.
Workshop Details

What Happens Inside the Workshop

Students don't just watch — they build, connect, program, test, and debug real hardware from day one. We teach everything from scratch. No prior knowledge needed.

Who Can Join
Open to students from Grade 5 to Grade 10. No prior knowledge of electronics or programming required — we start from absolute zero.
Hardware Kits
We bring all kits to your school. Students work with real microcontrollers, sensors, and actuators — the school needs to provide nothing.
What They Learn
Circuit connections, programming logic, sensor interfacing, output testing, and debugging — all hands-on. Students also get their first exposure to a real programming language.
Certification
Every student receives an official Roboden certificate on completing the workshop — a recognition of their real engineering work.
What Students Build
8 Mini Projects
Sensors · LEDs · Displays · Buzzers
1 Major Project
A complete working system combining all concepts
Roboden Certificate
Issued to every student who completes the workshop. A real achievement, not just a participation trophy.

From Grade 1 to Grade 10, every student gets access to hands-on lab practicals in a structured, enjoyable way. We don't just install equipment — we stay with you through the entire academic year.
